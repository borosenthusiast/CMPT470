About Us

Match Paws is ALL about you and your pet. Whether you have one or are looking for your new best friend, we're here to help!

Our team came together to help both owners and their animals become the best they can be. Our matching system will help you find your new best friend. And if you already have a dog, show him/her off in our friendly community! MatchPaws will forge an unbreakable bond between you and your pet.
